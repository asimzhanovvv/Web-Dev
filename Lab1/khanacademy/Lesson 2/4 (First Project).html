Welcome to the Majestic Tatra Mountains!

The Polish Tatras are one of Europe's most breathtaking mountain ranges.
Rugged peaks, emerald lakes and lush valleys make this destination a paradise for hikers, climbers and nature lovers alike.
Explore serene alpine meadows, crystal lakes, panoramic viewpoints, and charming mountain towns like Zakopane — the gateway to unforgettable adventures.

Whether you're walking easy valley trails, riding cable cars to towering peaks,
or discovering peaceful waterfalls and caves, the Tatras offer something magical for every traveler.

Top Places to Visit

  • Morskie Oko - stunning alpine lake surrounded by peaks
  • Kasprowy Wierch - peak reachable by cable car with panoramic views
  • Chochołowska Glade - vast meadow famous for purple crocuses in spring
  • Giewont - iconic peak overlooking Zakopane
  • Valleys of Five Lakes - beautiful alpine lakes and hiking routes
  • Thermal Baths - relax after hiking with mountain views

Things to Do

  • Hiking scenic trails of Tatra National Park
  • Ride cable car up to Kasprowy Wierch
  • Explore Morskie Oko lake on foot
  • Relax in thermal spas near Zakopane
  • Visit local mountain museums and cultural spots
